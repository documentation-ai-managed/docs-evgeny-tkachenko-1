## Overview

Evgeny Tkachenko provides a centralized documentation space where you can organize, create, and manage all your project documentation efficiently. You get full control over structuring content with Markdown, MDX components, and custom layouts. This platform supports collaboration, version history, and search to keep your teams aligned.

Whether you document APIs, user guides, or internal wikis, Evgeny Tkachenko scales with your needs.

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes with these essential steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your First Page" icon="file-plus">
    Navigate to the dashboard and click "New Page". Choose a template or start blank.

    ```bash
    # Example Markdown structure for your first doc
    # my-project.mdx
    ---
    title: My Project Overview
    ---
    
    ## Getting Started
    Welcome to your project!
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Content" icon="edit-3">
    Write in Markdown or MDX. Preview changes live as you edit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish & Share" icon="share-2">
    Save and publish. Share the link or embed in your tools.
  </Step>
</Steps>
