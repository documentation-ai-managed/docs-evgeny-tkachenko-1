## Overview

Organize docs by projects for easier management.

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Visit `https://docs.evgenytkachenko.com` and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start new project">
    Click the "New Project" button on your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your project">
    Enter a clear, descriptive name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add content">
    Create sections or pages within the project as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite team members">
    Use the "Invite" button to add collaborators.
  </Step>
</Steps>
