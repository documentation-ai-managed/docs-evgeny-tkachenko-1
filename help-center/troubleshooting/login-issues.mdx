## Issue
- "Login failed" or stuck on sign-in page

## Possible Reasons
- Wrong email/password
- Network error

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your credentials">
    Make sure your email and password are correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    Use the "Forgot Password" link to reset it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your internet connection">
    Retry on a stable network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Still blocked?">
    Contact support for manual help.
  </Step>
</Steps>
